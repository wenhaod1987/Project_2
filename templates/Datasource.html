RedFin Housing Data

RedFin is a real estate brokerage which gathers and publishes information on the housing market. The RedFin database gave us a quick and easy way to pull free and accurate housing information in csv format. From this dataset, we had access to housing prices, inventory, and sales. The dataseet is organized by zip code and was integrated into MongoDB for this project.
